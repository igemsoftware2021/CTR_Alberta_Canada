How we came up with our Tree Planting Calculator

For our tree planting calculator, we based our formula around values found on Tentree. Tentree’s website states that “46 trees will absorb 1 tonne of CO2 per year.” Thus, to find the tonnes of CO2 that 1 tree can absorb per year, we divided 1 tonne by 46 trees to attain a value of 0.022 tonnes/tree. We fact checked this amount on websites such as 10 Million Trees which states that “a mature tree absorbs carbon dioxide at a rate of 48 pounds per year” which is equal to about 0.022 tonnes per year. Since our carbon calculator derives a final CO2 emissions value in kg, we then converted 0.022 tonnes to kg.

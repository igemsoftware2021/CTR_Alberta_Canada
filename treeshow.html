How we came up with our Tree Planting Calculator

This calculator is based upon an assumption that trees take in 25kg of carbon dioxide per year. Hence, in this calculator, we show you the amount of trees growing over the period of one year needed to offset your emissions. So basically, to offset your emissions you must plant the trees, and once each one of your trees is one year old your emissions are offsetted!

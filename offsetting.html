Offsetting Guide

Offsetting Guide

Tree Canada

Tree Canada is the leading national tree planting charity in Canada, dedicated to helping individuals, communities, and businesses leave a positive impact on the environment. They provide offsetting opportunities through donations that will go towards planting trees.

TenTree

Tentree is a sustainable clothing brand, dedicated to finding and sourcing materials that create a smaller ecological footprint. For every item sold, Tentree plants ten trees in developing countries to rehabilitate natural ecosystems alongside removing C02 from the atmosphere. Donations can be made to their partnering non-profit organizations to further aid Tentrees's commitment to "Earth First" where their mission is "Making choices in a world where the planet and its people come first, always".

Gold Standard

Gold standard is committed and believes in climate security, sustainable development for all, and reductions of GHG emissions within the environment. They are attempting to reduce carbon emissions through new and old ways, and hope to reach the global goals through verified means.

Climate Action Reserve

Climate reserve action attempts to develop, promote and support more innovative and sustainable climate change solutions. They work together with other organizations to achieve a means of climate change solution which will benefit ecosystems, economy, and society.

Expedition Air

Expedition Air mitigates and offsets climate change through their “Made from Air” products and “Carbon Offsets”. While checking out, the additional purchase of carbon credits can be used to fund partnered projects of Avani, through Expedition Air. These partnered projects are approved by Gold Standard and Verified Carbon Standard and sequester carbon dioxides such as Carbontech, deforestation projects, and fuel-efficient stoves.

Native Energy

Native energy is committed to making a change environmentally and having an impact on reducing carbon emissions. They are transparent about their energy uses and commit to making the GHG emissions go down. They have also developed many projects regarding a reduction in GHG emissions.

How we came up with our Carbon Calculator

For our carbon calculator, we based our formula around different resources. We used the website "Carbonfootprint" to derive a value for the kg of CO2 that are produced per km during a flight. Carbon Footprint provided data for the kg of CO2 that are produced per km for different kinds of planes. We took the average of all of these amounts in order to attain a universal value for a plane. We then converted this value into kg, and multiplied it by 2 so that the value could represent a roundtrip.

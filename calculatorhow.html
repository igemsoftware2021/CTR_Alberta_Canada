How we came up with our Carbon Calculator

The CO2 calculator formula was derived from a mathematical calculation that took the amount of simple jet fuel or kerosene and converted it into the mass of carbon dioxide produced. We used a constant of about 600 liters of jet fuel used per 100 kilometers, in which using the mass of carbon dioxide produced per liter for kerosene, derived our formula of what mass of carbon dioxide is produced within a kilometer. Then to confirm our formula, we found numerous other values that were very similar to ours, confirming that our calculation was correct. Then within the app the user inputs the distance that they traveled and using the constant, it will calculate the total carbon dioxide emissions produced.

How we chose our offsets

Criteria we used to choose our companies includes if the company is transitioning towards increased sustainable methods in energy and waste, actively attempting to reduce their current emissions, and if this corporation supports environmental and offsetting initiatives. Our criteria includes if this corporation is sponsored by environmentally conscious groups or projects and if the corporation sponsors any non-governmental organizations. Also included within our criteria is if this corporation is looking forward to future projects on increasing their sustainability and if they are actively attempting to create more environmentally conscious products. Our criteria includes if the company has a sustainable means of conserving energy and is transparent about where their energy comes from and how it is being utilized.
